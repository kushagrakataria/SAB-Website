About the founder

Rudraksh Bhandari

Founder, ShareAllBooks

Rudraksh, founder of ShareAllBooks, is committed to making education accessible. Originally from Himachal Pradesh, India and now based in the UAE, he developed ShareAllBooks to connect book owners with those in need. Heading to UC San Diego for Computer Science, he aims to expand ShareAllBooks globally, fostering a supportive community for students and book lovers.
